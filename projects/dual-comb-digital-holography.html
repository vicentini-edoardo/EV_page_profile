First demonstration of dual-comb spectroscopy applied to digital holography, enabling simultaneous high-resolution spectroscopy and three-dimensional imaging.

Why it matters

First demonstration of dual-comb spectroscopy applied to digital holography, enabling simultaneous high-resolution spectroscopy and three-dimensional imaging. This work supports rigorous, reproducible nano-optics experiments and helps translate measurements into device-relevant insights.
