Polariton dynamics in nanocavities

Near-field mapping of plasmon and phonon polariton lifetimes and propagation.

Outcome

Resolved polariton propagation vectors and lifetimes in nanocavity architectures with nanoscale precision.

Why it matters

Delivers quantitative insight for engineering strong and ultrastrong coupling regimes in nanophotonic devices.

What was built

  • Near-field imaging workflow for polariton dispersion
  • Analysis pipeline for group velocity extraction
  • Data-driven modeling of cavity coupling strength

Methods

s-SNOM mapping, dispersion extraction, coupled-mode analysis, finite-element simulation.

Outputs

  • Journal publication on polariton lifetimes
  • Conference presentation on ultrastrong coupling
  • Open dataset with propagation maps

Timeline

2022-2024 - Next phase: hybrid plasmon-phonon platforms.
