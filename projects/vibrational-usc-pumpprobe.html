Vibrational strong–ultrastrong coupling by pump–probe nanospectroscopy

2023–present

Real-space observation and control of vibrational strong and ultrastrong coupling at the nanoscale using femtosecond pump–probe near-field spectroscopy.

Why it matters

Real-space observation and control of vibrational strong and ultrastrong coupling at the nanoscale using femtosecond pump–probe near-field spectroscopy. This work supports rigorous, reproducible nano-optics experiments and helps translate measurements into device-relevant insights.

My role

Project lead; experimental design, pump–probe implementation, data analysis and interpretation

Methods

  • pump–probe nanospectroscopy
  • ultrafast near-field microscopy
  • strong and ultrastrong coupling analysis
  • time-resolved spectral mapping
