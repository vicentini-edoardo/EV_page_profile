Dual-comb surface metrology

A frequency-comb readout chain for absolute calibration of nanoscale spectra.

Outcome

Delivered comb-referenced nano-FTIR spectra with traceable frequency accuracy across the mid-IR.

Why it matters

Provides quantitative, reproducible spectral measurements that can be compared across labs and instruments.

What was built

  • Dual-comb synchronization and referencing hardware
  • Calibration transfer protocol for near-field spectra
  • Data pipeline for coherent averaging and resampling

Methods

Dual-comb spectroscopy, frequency referencing, system identification, coherent averaging.

Outputs

  • Peer-reviewed publication on comb calibration
  • Open-source calibration scripts
  • Cross-lab benchmark dataset

Timeline

2024-2025 - Future work includes faster acquisition and shared calibration standards.
