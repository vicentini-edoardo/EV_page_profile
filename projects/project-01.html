Ultrafast nano-FTIR platform

A modular pump-probe near-field microscope delivering femtosecond-resolved vibrational spectra.

Outcome

Achieved phase-resolved pump-probe nano-FTIR measurements with nanometer spatial resolution and sub-10 fs timing stability.

Why it matters

Enables direct observation of ultrafast energy flow at surfaces, informing the design of active nano-photonic devices.

What was built

  • Timing-stabilized pump-probe interferometer
  • Phase-resolved heterodyne detection pipeline
  • Automated measurement scripts and calibration routines

Methods

s-SNOM, nano-FTIR, femtosecond pump-probe timing, lock-in phase detection, spectral deconvolution.

Outputs

  • Journal submission on phase-resolved nano-FTIR dynamics
  • Conference tutorial on ultrafast near-field instrumentation
  • Open analysis toolkit for pump-probe mapping

Timeline

2025-ongoing - Current milestones include expanded wavelength coverage and automation for high-throughput scans.
