Why it matters

Near-field imaging and dispersion analysis of terahertz plasmon polaritons in novel materials, revealing propagation, confinement, and damping mechanisms at THz frequencies. This work supports rigorous, reproducible nano-optics experiments and helps translate measurements into device-relevant insights.
