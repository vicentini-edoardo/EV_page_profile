Multifrequency s-SNOM for nano-optical imaging

2022–2023

Development of multifrequency scattering-type near-field microscopy schemes enabling simultaneous nano-imaging at multiple optical and infrared frequencies.

Why it matters

Development of multifrequency scattering-type near-field microscopy schemes enabling simultaneous nano-imaging at multiple optical and infrared frequencies. This work supports rigorous, reproducible nano-optics experiments and helps translate measurements into device-relevant insights.

My role

Method development and experimental implementation

Methods

  • multifrequency near-field detection
  • pseudo-heterodyne interferometry
  • harmonic demodulation
  • nano-optical contrast mechanisms
