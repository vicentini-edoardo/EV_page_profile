I am an experimental physicist at CIC nanoGUNE (Nanooptics Group). I develop instrumentation that merges near-field nano-imaging with ultrafast timing and frequency-comb precision to turn surface light–matter interaction into quantitative, reproducible measurements — not just compelling images.

Research focus

Nano-optics & polaritons

Near-field imaging (s-SNOM, nano-FTIR) of phonon- and plasmon-polaritons across the mid-IR to THz, resolving confinement and dissipation far below the wavelength.

Ultrafast spectroscopy & frequency combs
